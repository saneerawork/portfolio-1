Active Sports Clubs Union Square San Francisco

Modern cardio and strength training equipment, one-on-one personal training sessions, an expansive indoor lap pool and a full slate of group fitness classes sit just steps away from your accommodations at Marines' Memorial Club and Hotel. Active Sports Clubs Union Square, the premier San Francisco gym, is located onsite at our hotel, offering complimentary access for our guests.

Hours:

5:30 a.m. to 10 p.m. Monday through Friday
7 a.m. to 7 p.m. Saturday and Sunday

Features of Active Sports Clubs Union Square San Francisco:

  • Cardio training equipment
  • Strength training equipment
  • Dedicated Pilates studio and group fitness classes
  • Indoor heated lap pool
  • Massage treatment room and steam rooms
  • Free day lockers, towels and toiletries; Private lockers available
  • Certified personal training service with one free assessment and training session
  • An ADA Accessible Pool Lift in the Active Sports Clubs Union Square for access to the swimming pool
MMC hotel guests must present their hospitality pass to sign-in with gym reception desk for access to the gym and schedule a time for the indoor “lap” pool.
