Take Advantage of Reciprocal Clubs and Global Partnerships

When you stay at the Marines’ Memorial Club Hotel, one of the most exclusive San Francisco private clubs, you’ll have a chance to learn about reciprocal clubs around the world. These global partnerships are available to all members of Marine Club San Francisco, giving members the opportunity to visit private clubs across the globe while still retaining all the perks that they receive at Marines’ Memorial Club in San Francisco.


Learn more about visiting reciprocal clubs and how to request a letter of introduction when you stay with Marines’ Memorial Club & Hotel, one of the best private clubs in San Francisco.



FAQ

How do I obtain reservations at a reciprocal club?

  • Contact the club of your choice either by email or telephone.
  • When making your reservation, please be prepared to provide your membership number and credit card information.
  • Inquire whether your Marines Memorial Club membership card is sufficient for guest privileges at the club or whether a letter of introduction is required.
  • Please note that when visiting International Clubs, your passport and membership card may be required.

I'll be a graduate student next year. May I apply?

The student must be the one to fill out the application. If an application is found to have been filled out by someone who is not the student, the applicant will be disqualified.

I'll be attending a trade or vocational school. May I apply?

The student must be the one to fill out the application. If an application is found to have been filled out by someone who is not the student, the applicant will be disqualified.

Letter of Introduction Request

In order to complete your request for a Letter of Introduction, you must:

  1. Be a member in good standing
  2. Login
  3. Be within 30 days of your visit to the prospective Reciprocal Club. If your membership has lapsed, please contact our membership department directly.

Please enter your approximate date of visit below and submit. A Letter of Introduction will be emailed to the club you chose, and you will receive a confirmation email. Please email Carol Taylor with any questions or comments at Caroltaylor@MarineClub.com.
