2017-2018 Scholarships

Applications for scholarships for the 2017-2018 school year are now closed. Please check back in October for application information for 2018-2019.
For more information on our scholarship program please read our FAQ

Deadline: April 21, 2017

Wesley Hammon Leach Scholarship

At least two (2) Wesley Hammon Leach Scholarships are available for the 2017-2018 school year. Each scholarship is $2,500.



Eligibility

To be eligible for the Wesley Hammon Leach Scholarship applicants must:

  • Attend a trade or vocational school accredited through the “Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC) (http://www.accsc.org/Directory/index.aspx) during the 2017/2018 school year.
  • Have a focus of study that will lead to a viable career; preference will be given to students with a medical or nursing focus.
  • Be either an active member of the Marines’ Memorial, a child or grandchild of a member, OR must be a student Veteran.

How to Apply

Required To Apply for the Wesley Hammon Leach Scholarship:

  • Complete the online application.
  • A copy of your most recent high school or trade school transcript.
  • 2 References must submit letters of recommendation by the deadline of April 21, 2017. (Letters of Reference  Instructions HERE.)
  • Upload your 250-500 word Original Essay on the following topic:
    - What is the best advice you have ever received? Who gave you the advice, and in what context?
    Did you follow it?

FAQ

Thank you for your interest in the Marines’ Memorial Scholarship Program! Below are the most frequently asked questions about the program and application process.
Due to the high volume of calls and emails about the Scholarship Program, we recommend you read the questions below before contacting the Marines’ Memorial staff.

I qualify for more than one scholarship type (i.e. Sgts Rose and Hallock Scholarships), can I apply for both?

The student must be the one to fill out the application. If an application is found to have been filled out by someone who is not the student, the applicant will be disqualified.

I'll be a graduate student next year. May I apply?

The student must be the one to fill out the application. If an application is found to have been filled out by someone who is not the student, the applicant will be disqualified.

I'll be attending a trade or vocational school. May I apply?

The student must be the one to fill out the application. If an application is found to have been filled out by someone who is not the student, the applicant will be disqualified.
