Meeting Planners Special

Make the Most of your Union Square Getaway


Consider the Marines' Memorial Club & Hotel to host your next event. Offering elegant spaces, attentive services, and value added amenities, our hotel welcomes groups with pleasure. Whether you are planning a board meeting or corporate retreat, our hotel in Union Square has everything you need to make it a memorable occasion.


For our overnight guests, our San Francisco hotel rooms offer all the amenities to make you feel at home.


We know location is important! The area surrounding our hotel is overflowing with culture and attractions synonymous with San Francisco. Make sure to look through our guide of some of the more popular destinations in the city, all within close proximity to the Club!
