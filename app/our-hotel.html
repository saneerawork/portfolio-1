We’re an Environment
Friendly Hotel

Our San Francisco hotel has been part of the community since 1946 displaying a commitment to the Club, its members, valued guests and the environment. We recognize the importance of preserving our environment and have taken numerous measures to be one of the leading green hotels in San Francisco. Our staff is committed to the conservation of the environment and they are continuously seeking new ways to improve our sustainability measures.

Please email us if you have any questions about our San Francisco Green Hotel Initiatives.
