Give to the Marines Memorial Association

The Marines’ Memorial Association depends on donations to fulfill our mission to commemorate, educate and serve. Donations may be made by anyone, including members, non-members, businesses, organizations and foundations. All donations made to Marines Memorial Association are tax deductible per the IRS because the Association complies fully with IRC 170(c)(3) of the US Tax Code, as an IRC 501(c)(19) Veterans Organization. Our Federal EIN is 94-1055967.

Gold Star Parents Event

Each year, the Marines' Memorial hosts a 2-day gathering for Gold Star Parents (parents whose sons or daughters died while serving in the US Armed Forces). The Event gives these parents a unique opportunity to come together and learn from one another while commemorating their lost children.

Active Duty Aid and Assist Fund

This program allows the Marines' Memorial to offer free memberships and drastically reduced Hotel room rates to Active Duty and Reservists in the US Armed Forces. It also includes Care Packages and other services provided to those currently serving.

Tribute Memorial Wall/Living Memorial Fund

Donations to the Living Memorial help maintain the memorabilia found throughout the Marines' Memorial Club. They also support our Tribute Memorial Wall which honors the young men and women who lost their lives while fighting in Iraq and Afghanistan. Located on the 10th Floor Mezzanine, the Wall is made up of thousands of black marble bricks, each paying tribute to one fallen warrior.
